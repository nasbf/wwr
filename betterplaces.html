Fonce River: Emotion in Santander

Santander is a paradise for adventure sports enthusiasts, and the Fonce River is one of its most precious treasures. With class III and IV rapids, this river offers an exciting adventure for those seeking an intermediate level of challenge. The crystal-clear waters and impressive natural environment make rafting on the Fonce River an unforgettable experience.

Descent of the Chicamocha River: Natural Beauty and Excitement

The Chicamocha River gives you the perfect combination of scenic beauty and exciting rapids. This river runs through the majestic Chicamocha Canyon, offering spectacular panoramic views while facing class IV rapids. It is an experience that awakens all your senses and immerses you in the wonder of Colombian nature.

Ubication

If you are in Colombia you can get to the Chicamocha Canyon in three different ways:

• Plane: by plane landing at the Palonegro international airport in Bucaramanga, which is the closest. Then you must take an intermunicipal bus to reach the municipalities of Destino Chicamocha.

• Private car: by land in a private car taking the Bucaramanga-Bogotá national highway, or Bucaramanga-Málaga if you are going to Molagavita or Capitanejo.

• Intermunicipal bus: by land by intermunicipal bus arriving in Bucaramanga and from there taking a bus to the different municipalities.

Suarez River: An Unforgettable Challenge

If you are looking for a truly challenging experience, the Suarez river is your ideal destination. With class V rapids, this river demands skill and courage. Rafting enthusiasts will experience intense emotions as they navigate turbulent waters and technical rapids. This is definitely a place for the bravest adventurers.

Ubication

Suarez river is located in the municipality of Barbosa is a municipality located in the department of Santander, at a distance of approximately 185 km from Bogotá. Traveling to this municipality has an estimated time of 3 hours 40 minutes, which may change depending on the point in the capital where the tour begins. The Bogotá Barbosa route is a route that has several unmissable landscapes and crosses some very culturally and gastronomically rich municipalities.
